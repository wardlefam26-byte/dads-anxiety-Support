Family Guide

Helpful language

“I can see this is really hard.”

“I'm here with you while this feeling passes.”

“We don't have to solve that uncertainty right now.”

“You stayed with something difficult.”

Try to avoid

“I promise nothing bad will happen.”

“Everything will definitely be fine.”

Repeatedly answering the same reassurance question.

Surprising, forcing, or arguing Dad into a feared situation.

Support Dad, not the anxiety. This app is not a diagnosis or emergency service.
